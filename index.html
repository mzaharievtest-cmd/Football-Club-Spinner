What is Football Spinner? How it works

What is Football Spinner?

Spin to guess Team, Stadium, or Player. Pick a sub-mode, spin, then hit Reveal.

How it works

Sub-modes switch what appears on the wheel (logo, stadium, name, photo, or club+flag+number). The sidebar filters still work with Team data. Free Spin uses whatever is currently built.
