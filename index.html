How it works

  • Guess Team → By Logo or By Stadium (+ League)
  • Guess Stadium → By Team Name or By Logo
  • Guess Player → By Photo or By Club + Flag + Number
  • Click SPIN, then Reveal to show the answer.
  • Confetti pops and a crowd cheer plays on reveal (audio can be muted in your browser tab if needed).
