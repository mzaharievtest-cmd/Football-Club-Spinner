What is Football Spinner?

Football Spinner is a free, fan-made random football club generator built for quick fun, discovery, and football trivia.

How it works

Choose leagues (or Premier League clubs in PLAYER mode), pick what to show on the wheel, then spin. If too many entries are selected, the wheel switches to wedge-only for performance.
