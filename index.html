What is Football Spinner?

Football Spinner is a free, fan-made tool that works as a football club spin the wheel and a random Premier League player generator. It’s built for quick fun, discovery and football trivia — no login, no paywall, just open the site and spin.

The idea is simple: instead of arguing for half an hour which match to watch or which club to use in a quiz, you let the wheel decide.

How it works

1. Pick a mode

Use the TEAM / PLAYER toggle at the top:

  • TEAM — spin among football clubs from 25 European leagues.
  • PLAYER — spin among Premier League players (loaded from players.json).

2. Filter what’s on the wheel

In TEAM mode you can filter by league:

  • All leagues — select everything that’s currently visible.
  • Clear — deselect all leagues.
  • Top 5 leagues — quickly focus on the big five (Premier League, La Liga, Serie A, Bundesliga, Ligue 1).

Extra leagues are hidden under “Show more leagues” so the wheel stays readable, especially on mobile.

3. Choose what to show on the wheel

Under “Show on wheel” you can toggle:

  • Name — club or player name inside the wedge.
  • Logo — club crest or player image.
  • Stadium — home ground (club mode).
  • League — league label for extra context.

When there are too many items, the app automatically hides some details to keep the wheel readable but still visually satisfying to spin.

4. Spin!

Click the big SPIN button or the floating action button beside the wheel. The pointer stops on a random wedge and the result appears both on the wheel and in a small modal with logo, league and stadium details.

Every result is added to History on the left so you can see which clubs or players have already appeared in this session.

Leagues covered

The TEAM wheel currently includes clubs from 25 European leagues, including:

  • England — Premier League, Championship
  • Spain — La Liga
  • Italy — Serie A
  • Germany — Bundesliga
  • France — Ligue 1
  • Plus many more domestic top flights and secondary leagues across Europe.

The exact list of leagues and clubs is defined in teams.json. As new seasons or competitions are added, that file can be updated without changing the core app.

Why use Football Spinner?

  • Trivia nights: use the wheel to pick random clubs or players for quiz questions.
  • Watch parties: let the wheel pick a team for you to support tonight.
  • Discovery: learn about clubs you normally wouldn’t follow.
  • Content ideas: streamers and TikTok creators can use spins as prompts for videos.

Because everything runs in the browser, there is nothing to install and it also works nicely on mobile.

FAQ

Is Football Spinner free?
Yes. The site is completely free to use. There is no login or registration.
Can I spin only one league?
Yes. Use the league chips to select just the league you want (for example only the Premier League) and then press SPIN.
Do you support national teams?
Right now the focus is on club football. National teams or other competitions can be added later if there is demand.
Where do player images come from?
Player images are loaded from the public /players directory or from external sources referenced in players.json. When an image is missing, the wheel shows a neutral silhouette.
