Football Spinner – spin the wheel for random football clubs and players

Football Spinner is a free random football team generator and Premier League player spinner. Instead of scrolling through long lists, you simply spin the wheel and let chance pick a club or player.

The app supports 25 European leagues in TEAM mode and a dedicated PLAYER mode focused on current Premier League squads. It’s built for quizzes, prediction games, TikTok content, watch parties and anyone who enjoys football and a bit of randomness.

How the football club & player spinner works

  1. Choose a mode – TEAM (clubs) or PLAYER (Premier League players).
  2. Filter what you want to include – pick leagues or Premier League teams.
  3. Decide what to show on the wheel – name, logo, stadium, league.
  4. Hit SPIN – the wheel uses a fair pseudo-random algorithm so each option has the same chance.
  5. Open the result modal to see extra details such as stadium and league.

Which leagues are included?

TEAM mode currently covers 25 European domestic leagues, including:

  • England – Premier League
  • Spain – LaLiga
  • Italy – Serie A
  • Germany – Bundesliga
  • France – Ligue 1
  • and many others such as Eredivisie, Scottish Premiership, Süper Lig, Ekstraklasa and more.

PLAYER mode focuses on active Premier League players, with player images, clubs and nationalities loaded from an up-to-date dataset.

Ideas & use cases

You can use Football Spinner for:

  • Football quizzes and pub trivia nights
  • Classroom games and geography activities around clubs and leagues
  • TikTok / YouTube Shorts content ideas (guess the club, blind rankings, challenges)
  • Picking a random team to support during a matchday
  • Creating prediction challenges with friends or stream viewers

FAQ

Is Football Spinner free?
Yes – the tool is completely free to use and does not require an account.

Do you store personal data?
No personal accounts are used. Basic analytics (via Google Analytics) may collect anonymous usage stats. Your league and mode preferences are stored locally in your browser only.

Can I use it on my phone?
Yes. The interface is responsive and works on mobile, tablet and desktop.

Updates & roadmap

The app is still evolving. Planned improvements include more leagues, better player filtering, additional quiz modes and quality-of-life updates for creators and teachers.
