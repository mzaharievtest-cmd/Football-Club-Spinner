What is Football Spinner?

Football Spinner is a free, fan‑made random football club generator built for quick fun, discovery, and football trivia. Choose the competitions you care about and spin to get one club at random from the biggest European leagues, including the Premier League, La Liga, Serie A, Bundesliga, and Ligue 1, plus many others. Each result can display the club’s name, logo/crest, league, and home stadium; you can also hide any of these to run a “guess the club” or “guess the stadium” round with friends. There’s no signup and nothing to install—just a fast, friendly tool that helps you discover teams, pick a neutral game to watch, or spark conversation. We’re independent and not affiliated with any club, league, or governing body. Ready to try your luck? Pick a few leagues and hit Spin.

How it works

  1. Pick leagues: Select one or many—stick to a single division or mix the big five for variety.
  2. Spin the wheel: We randomly select one club and show a clean result card.
  3. Reveal or guess: Toggle the logo, league, and stadium on/off to run quick quiz rounds, then reveal the answer.

Optimized for phones and tablets—great between matches or on the go.
