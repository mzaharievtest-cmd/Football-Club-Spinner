About Football Spinner

Football Spinner is a free tool for fans to explore football clubs and players in a fun, interactive way. Spin the wheel to discover new teams from 25 European leagues or get a random Premier League player for your next quiz.

How it works

Choose your filters: in TEAM mode pick leagues (25 European leagues supported); in PLAYER mode pick Premier League teams. Set what to show on the wheel and hit Spin. The wheel lands on a random result.

Why use it?

It makes football discovery fun again — ideal for trivia nights, deciding who to support, or learning about new clubs and players. Free, fast, and no login required.

What is Football Spinner?

Football Spinner is a football club spin the wheel that lets you get a random football team from 25 European leagues or a random Premier League player.

How do I spin the wheel?

Select your leagues on the left, choose what you want to show on the wheel, then click Spin. The wheel stops on a random result and adds it to your history.

Can I spin only Premier League teams?

Yes. In TEAM mode you can select only the Premier League from the league chips. In PLAYER mode the wheel already focuses on Premier League players.

Is Football Spinner free to use?

Yes, Football Spinner is completely free and works in the browser with no login or registration.

What can I use Football Spinner for?

You can use it for football quizzes, watch party challenges, choosing a random team for career modes, or content ideas for TikTok, YouTube Shorts and streams.
