About Football Spinner

Football Spinner is a free tool for fans to explore football clubs and players in a fun, interactive way. Spin the wheel to discover new teams, test your knowledge, or decide who to support next matchday.

How it works

Choose your leagues or clubs, select what to show on the wheel, and hit Spin.

The wheel will pick a random team or player - perfect for trivia nights, football debates, or discovering new favorites.
