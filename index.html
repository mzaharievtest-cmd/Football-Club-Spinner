What is Football Spinner?

Football Spinner is a free, fan‑made random football club generator built for quick fun, discovery, and football trivia. Pick the competitions you care about and spin to get one club at random from Europe’s biggest leagues — including the Premier League, La Liga, Serie A, Bundesliga, and Ligue 1. Each result can display the club’s name, logo/crest, league, and home stadium; hide any of them to run a “guess the club” or “guess the stadium” round with friends. No sign‑up, nothing to install, and no affiliation with any club, league, or governing body — just a fast, friendly tool to discover teams, pick a neutral game to watch, or spark conversation. Choose a few leagues and hit Spin.

How it works

  1. Pick leagues: Select one or many—stick to a single division or mix the big five for variety.
  2. Spin the wheel: We randomly select one club and show a clean result card.
  3. Reveal or guess: Toggle the logo, league, and stadium on/off to run quick quiz rounds, then reveal the answer.

Optimized for phones and tablets — change leagues anytime to narrow or broaden the pool.
