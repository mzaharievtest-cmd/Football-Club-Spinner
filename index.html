About Football Spinner

About Football Spinner

Football Spinner is a free tool for fans to explore football clubs and players in a fun, interactive way. Spin the wheel to discover new teams from 25 European leagues or get a random Premier League player for your next quiz.

How it works

How it works

Choose your filters: in TEAM mode pick leagues; in PLAYER mode pick Premier League teams. Set what to show on the wheel and hit Spin.

Why use it?

Why use it?

Perfect for trivia nights, deciding who to support, or learning about new clubs and players. Free, fast, and no login required.
